About Outprint

Our Mission

Outprint is building a distributed manufacturing network designed to make high-quality 3D printing more accessible and affordable. By connecting customers with a community of reliable, independent makers, we're making it easier to turn digital designs into real-world partsâ€”on-demand and on your terms.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional manufacturing is slow, expensive, and centralized. We believe that local, flexible production should be the future. Whether it's a prototype, replacement part, or functional end-use item, customers deserve faster turnaround, better pricing, and direct access to skilled producers. That's what we're here to deliver.

Our Approach

We're starting simply-using integrated forms to collect applications and order requests-because we working on creating connections rather than launching with unnecessary complexity before it is even needed. Every feature we build is based on feedback from the people who contribute: the makers and customers using the platform.

Where We're Going

As the Outprint network grows, so will the tools behind it. We're planning seamless quote generation, printer matchmaking based on performance and availability, transparent turnaround times, and automatic fulfillment pipelines.

Who Should Join

If you're a 3D printer owner with reliable equipment and a maker's mindset, we want to hear from you. If you're a product designer, hobbyist, startup, engineer, or educator in need of custom parts then you're in the right place. Our system is built to prioritize quality, communication, and trust.

Join the First Wave

By joining early, you're not just gaining access to a growing customer base-you're helping shape a platform built for the future of manufacturing.
