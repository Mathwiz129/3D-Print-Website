Place an Order

How It Works

assignment Submit Your Order

Provide your 3d files and detailed part specifications. Include material preferences, quantity, and any special instructions.

search Printer Matching

Your request will be sent to verified 3D printers, and the best match will be selected based on availability and specifications.

construction Manufacturing

Once accepted, the printer will begin production. You'll receive an estimated completion time before printing begins.

local_shipping Shipping & Tracking

The completed part must be shipped within three days of production finishing. Tracking details will be provided upon dispatch.

payments Secure Payment Processing

A few days after delivery, payment will be processed, covering material costs and production fees.
