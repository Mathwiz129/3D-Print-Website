How It Works

build Share Your Capabilities

Tell us about your 3D printer setup, available materials, color options, and quality standards. This helps us match you with the right customers.

search Order Matching

Once approved, you'll be notified of available orders. If a customer request fits your capabilities, you'll be considered to fulfill the job.

check_circle Printer Selection

Based on availability, pricing, and customer needs, one printer is selected to complete the order. If chosen, you'll receive full details.

local_shipping Shipping & Delivery

Once the part is produced, you must ship within three days of estimated completion, ensuring safe and trackable delivery.

payments Payment Processing

A few days after successful delivery, payment will be sent, covering material costs and machine time fees based on the agreed pricing structure.
